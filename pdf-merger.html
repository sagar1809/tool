How to Merge PDF Files

  1. Click "Select Files" or drag and drop PDF files into the upload area
  2. Arrange files in your preferred order (optional)
  3. Choose compression and image optimization settings
  4. Click "Merge PDFs" to combine your files
  5. Download your merged PDF file
